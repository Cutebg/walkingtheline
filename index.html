Welcome

The Bellarose pump is a striking fusion of contemporary flair and timeless elegance, perfect for those who want to make a statement. With its 100 mm chandelier heel, the shoe commands attention from the first step, offering both height and drama. The heel is sculptural, bold, and uniquely designed to evoke the intricate beauty of a chandelier, setting it apart from traditional pumps.

The shoe's pointed toe adds a sharp, sophisticated edge, enhancing the overall sleek and modern silhouette. Crafted from a luxurious blend of black crepe satin and lurex, the materials exude refinement and subtle shine, creating a perfect balance between elegance and boldness. The shimmer of the lurex catches the light, making every movement a moment to behold.

The signature red sole completes the look, marking exclusivity and style while adding iconic luxury to the captivating design. Modeled after the famous Kate pump, the Bellarose honors the Maison's legacy of fashionable and impactful shoes. This pump is ideal for anyone wanting to elevate their wardrobe with a piece that exudes confidence and sophistication.
